We Look Forward To Serving You In Sending Many Items To Your Friends And Family In Cuba, With Trackable, Home Delivery Everywhere On The Island Of Cuba.

We are a Cuban American and American operated business created to help the citizens of Cuba get the necessary, vital products that they need, in the easiest and most cost-effective means possible.

We are not affiliated with the Cuban Government nor any Government owned businesses.

We only provide products to the Citizens of Cuba, purchased by their friends and family in the U.S. We then deliver those boxes to the homes of the recipients everywhere on the island of Cuba.
